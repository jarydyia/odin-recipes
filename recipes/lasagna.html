Lasagna

Description

This lasagna is very cheesey and has a bunch of flat noodles

It can be very hot so be careful

Ingredients

  • meat
  • cheese
  • red sauce
  • noodles

Steps

  1. put noodle down
  2. put sauce down
  3. put cheese and meat on top
  4. repeat for 3 layers
  5. heat at 500 degrees for 10 minutes
  6. and enjoy
